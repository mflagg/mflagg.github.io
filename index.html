Principal & Founder

Matt Flagg, Ph.D.

Matt Flagg received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Georgia Tech in 2010, specializing in computer vision, graphics and human-computer interaction.

Before consulting, he co-founded PlayMotion, a gesture-recognition company that deployed hundreds of projector-camera systems across hospitals, museums and family entertainment centers, including three divisions of Disney parks. He also contributed to a 3D scanning system honored with a Technical Achievement Academy Award at Industrial Light & Magic.

Today Flagg is Chief Science Officer at Code19 Racing — the world's first professional autonomous motorsports team — and leads Flagg Visual Computing, collaborating with longtime partner Dr. Satya Mallick (CEO of OpenCV). All client IP is owned by the customer.
